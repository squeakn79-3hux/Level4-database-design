# Introduction

Bright Sparks Ltd is a fictional Auckland-based electronics retailer specialising in the sale of consumer electronics, computer hardware, accessories and home technology products. The company was created as part of this project to provide a realistic business environment for designing and implementing a relational database solution.

As the business has grown, so has the volume of products, suppliers and customer transactions. Managing this information using manual processes has become increasingly inefficient, leading to difficulties in maintaining accurate inventory records and producing meaningful business reports.

To improve operational efficiency, Bright Sparks Ltd requires a centralised database capable of storing, organising and retrieving business information in a reliable and structured manner.

---

# Company Operations

Bright Sparks Ltd purchases products from multiple suppliers before selling them to customers through its retail operation.

The business is responsible for:

- Managing supplier information.
- Recording product details.
- Monitoring inventory levels.
- Processing customer sales.
- Producing management reports.
- Maintaining accurate stock records.

Each of these activities generates valuable business data that must be stored securely and remain easily accessible for day-to-day operations.

---

# Business Challenges

Prior to implementing the database system, the company faced several operational challenges.

These included:

- Time-consuming manual record keeping.
- Difficulty tracking inventory levels.
- Inconsistent supplier information.
- Limited reporting capabilities.
- Increased risk of data duplication.
- Reduced visibility of sales performance.

These issues highlighted the need for a structured relational database capable of improving both operational efficiency and decision-making.

---

# Proposed Solution

The proposed solution was the development of a relational database using MySQL with a graphical user interface developed in LibreOffice Base.

The system centralises all business information within a single database while maintaining data integrity through relationships, primary keys and foreign keys.

The completed solution enables staff to:

- Record new products.
- Maintain supplier information.
- Process sales transactions.
- Track inventory levels.
- Generate business reports.
- Analyse sales trends.
- Monitor stock availability.

---

# Expected Benefits

Implementing the Inventory Management Database System provides several benefits to Bright Sparks Ltd.

These include:

- Improved data accuracy.
- Reduced duplication of information.
- Faster access to business records.
- Better inventory management.
- More informed purchasing decisions.
- Improved reporting and business intelligence.
- Increased operational efficiency.

The solution provides a scalable foundation that could be expanded to support future business growth.

---

# Conclusion

Bright Sparks Ltd represents a realistic business scenario that demonstrates how database technologies can solve common operational problems faced by small and medium-sized businesses.

By replacing manual record-keeping processes with a structured relational database, the company gains improved control over its inventory, suppliers and sales information while supporting better decision-making through reliable and accessible business data.
